Menu

From traditional favorites to innovative creations, our menu offers something for every palate

    Appetizers:
  1. Paneer Tikka: Marinated cottage cheese cubes grilled to perfection.
  2. Vegetable Pakora: Crispy fritters made from assorted vegetables and chickpea flour.
  3. Samosa: Triangular pastries filled with spiced potatoes and peas, served with tangy tamarind chutney.
    Main Courses:
  1. Dal Makhani: Creamy black lentils simmered with butter and spices.
  2. Palak Paneer: Soft cubes of paneer in a rich spinach gravy.
  3. Vegetable Biryani: Fragrant basmati rice cooked with mixed vegetables and aromatic spices.
    Breads:
  1. Naan: Traditional Indian flatbread baked in a tandoor oven.
  2. Garlic Naan: Naan bread infused with garlic and fresh coriander.
  3. Roti: Whole wheat bread cooked on a griddle.
    Desserts:
  1. Gulab Jamun: Soft milk dumplings soaked in sugar syrup, served warm.
  2. Rasgulla: Spongy cottage cheese balls cooked in light syrup.
  3. Kulfi: Creamy Indian ice cream flavored with cardamom and pistachios.
    Beverages:
  1. Mango Lassi: Refreshing yogurt-based drink blended with ripe mangoes.
  2. Masala Chai: Spiced Indian tea brewed with aromatic spices and milk.
  3. Fresh Lime Soda: Tangy lime juice mixed with soda and a hint of sweetness.
